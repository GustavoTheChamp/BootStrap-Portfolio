    Our Story

    Colombian House is a Latin American food restaurant, founded by a young and entrepreneur Colombian couple, located in New Rochelle & White Plains, New York; one of the biggest and challenging city, which was not an obstacle to fulfill their dreams. The New Rochelle location opened its doors to the customer on May 2017; White Plains was then added in October 2022.

    After arduous years of work and effort, These two founders managed to launch their great dream of getting to share the wide and exquisite Colombian gastronomy with the world.
